The upcoming American superhero movie based on the comics of the same name by DC Comics. The picture will be released by Warner Bros. And will be a reload of all Batman movies.

The film is the tenth film in the DC Expanded Universe. The director and screenwriter of the film will be Matt Reeves, and the main role will be played by Robert Pattinson.

Development

In October 2014, Warner Bros. Studio announced that a solo film about Batman is under development, the role of which will again be played by Ben Affleck. In July 2015, it was reported that Affleck was in talks with the studio over the production of the film and co-writing the script with Jeff Jones.

After the release of Batman vs. Superman: At the Dawn of Justice, WME head Patrick Whitesel confirmed that Affleck had written a script for a solo film about Batman and was currently being watched by the studio and DC. In May 2016, Jeremy Irons confirmed that he would appear in the movie about the Dark Knight.

At the Comic-Con in San Diego in July 2016, it was officially confirmed that Affleck would become the director of the film. In August 2016, a test material was published on one of Affleck's pages on social networks, in which the character of Detrook was shown. On September 8, 2016, Jeff Jones confirmed that Joe Mangagnello will play the mercenary Slade Wilson, who may become the main villain of the film.
